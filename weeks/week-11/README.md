## 🏆 Competition Day Strategy

### Time Management (3-hour contest)
1. **First 10 min:** Read ALL problems. Categorize by difficulty.
2. **Next 90 min:** Solve the 5 math problems (18 min each).
3. **Next 70 min:** Solve easy coding problems first (A, B type).
4. **Final 10 min:** Review answers, check for silly mistakes.

### Problem Selection
- **Do easy problems first.** Every solved problem counts.
- **Don't get stuck.** If no progress after 10 min, skip and return.
- **Partial credit matters.** Submit what you have.

### Mental Game
- If you're stuck: take 3 deep breaths, re-read the problem.
- If you're behind: stay calm. One good solve can change everything.
- Trust the 10 weeks of preparation behind you.
