## Note-Taking Tips

1. **Write in your own words** — don't just copy formulas
2. **Include examples** — attach a solved problem to each concept
3. **Use diagrams** — visual explanations stick better
4. **Review regularly** — re-read notes at least once a week
5. **Update as you learn** — add new insights and correct mistakes
