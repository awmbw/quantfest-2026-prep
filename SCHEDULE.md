## Phase-Specific Adjustments

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1–3): Foundations
- Emphasis on **understanding concepts** over speed
- USACO modules: read thoroughly, solve all practice problems
- Green Book: spend extra time on solution explanations
- CSES: Introductory + Sorting & Searching problems

### Phase 2 (Weeks 4–6): Deep Dive
- Start timing yourself on Green Book problems (15 min max each)
- USACO: Silver completion + Gold Math & DP
- CSES: Dynamic Programming + Mathematics
- Green Book: Ch.4 Probability (the most important chapter)

### Phase 3 (Weeks 7–9): Peak & Taper
- Weekly mock tests
- CSES: Counting Problems + Additional math problems
- Focus on **problem identification** — quickly categorizing problem types
- Week 9: reduce volume, review weak areas, mental prep
