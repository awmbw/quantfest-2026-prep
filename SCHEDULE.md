# 📅 Daily Study Schedule — Quantfest 2026

> Suggested daily routine. Adjust times to fit your schedule.  
> Total: ~2.5–3 hours/day, 6 days/week (1 rest day).

---

| *Break* | 10 min | — | — |
| Block 3 | 30 min | 🧩 **Puzzles** | Brainstellar, Jane Street, QuantQuestions |

## Phase-Specific Adjustments

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1–3): Foundations
- Emphasis on **understanding concepts** over speed
- Spend extra time reading Green Book solution explanations
- Do easier problems to build confidence

### Phase 2 (Weeks 4–6): Deep Dive
- Start timing yourself on Green Book problems (15 min max each)
- Increase CF problem difficulty to 1400–1600
- Begin reviewing past Quantfest problems

### Phase 3 (Weeks 7–9): Integration
- Add weekly mock tests
- Focus on **problem identification** — quickly categorizing problem types
- Speed drills: rapid-fire easy problems

### Phase 4 (Weeks 10–11): Peak & Taper
- Reduce volume, increase quality
- Focus on review over new material
- Mental preparation and logistics
